# Business-analytics-
The data shows that ₹35 and ₹40 both generate the highest revenue (₹2,800), but ₹35 is the better choice
since it sells more bottles (80 vs. 70) for the same total earnings. As price increases, revenue rises
at first because the drop in demand is outweighed by the higher price per bottle, but this effect
plateaus around ₹35–₹40 beyond this point, further price hikes are unlikely to help. The shop owner
should therefore price the juice at ₹35 per bottle to maximize revenue while still selling a
healthy volume.
